We will be using a database backend for a website titled "Digital PCs" in order to catalog customer purchases and order history. The main purpose of this system/website is to enable customers to purchase PC parts and accessories to build their customized dream desktop. We required this to be hosted as a back-end database because we can store customer information and thus it allows us to have proper communication with our customers and it gives us data on our products, such as which of our products are being sold more or less. Digital PCs sell $1 million of PC parts annually. In our company, this translates to approximately 1200 parts being sold by the year.
